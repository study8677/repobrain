# LuxeMaurice — Private Wealth & Lifestyle Platform

**Strategic Vision 2030 — Presentation Outline**

*A 14‑slide presentation companion to `LUXEMAURICE_STRATEGIC_VISION_2030.md`.*

---

## Purpose of this outline

A boardroom‑grade walkthrough of the LuxeMaurice strategic vision, intended for a private session with the principals. Each slide carries a single, memorable message, an editorial visual direction, and suggested speaker copy.

Aesthetic across the deck: spacious, image‑led, restrained typography, a deep neutral palette, and one strong photograph or composition per slide. Charts are rare and only used where they earn their place.

---

- **Visual direction:** Full‑bleed editorial photograph — an early‑morning Mauritian coastline or a quiet architectural interior at dawn. No people. Title in fine serif, generous negative space.

## Deck‑wide notes for the design team

- **Palette:** deep neutral base (warm graphite, charcoal, ivory), one signature accent reserved for headings and dividers. Avoid bright primaries.
- **Typography:** a single refined serif for titles, a single quiet sans for supporting copy. Two type sizes per slide as a maximum.
- **Imagery:** real, atmospheric, never staged. Mauritius first; restraint always. People rarely, and never as the focus.
- **Negative space:** treat blank space as the most expensive material on the slide.
- **Motion:** none, or a single slow fade. Stillness signals confidence.
- **Length:** the deck should finish in under twenty minutes spoken. If a slide does not earn its place, remove it.
